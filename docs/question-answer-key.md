# PNB Sahayak — Question & Expected-Answer Key

The assistant's actual English answer to each question, with the genuine source it cites. (Ask in any of the 11 languages — see question-bank.md — the answer content is the same, spoken back in that language.)


## 1. When must staff pensioners submit their life certificate?
**Answer:** Staff pensioners must submit their life certificate from 1st November 2020 onwards till 31st December 2020.

**Source:** Extension of period for submission of Life Certificate till February 2021 by Staff Pensioners . (PPF132020final.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High

## 2. Is TDS deducted from a pensioner's pension income?
**Answer:** Yes, TDS is deducted from a pensioner's pension income, and investment details must be submitted to get exemptions.

**Source:** TAX DEDUCTION AT SOURCE (TDS) FROM PENSION INCOME OF PENSIONERS FOR FY2019-20 AND RECORDING OF INVESTMENT DETAILS IN HRMS (Investment.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High

## 3. What schemes are covered under the Staff Welfare Fund?
**Answer:** The Staff Welfare Fund covers schemes such as canteen facilities, scholarships for employees' children, financial aid for employees who die in service, assistance for employees with mentally retarded children, and adjustment of outstanding housing loans in case of employee death.

**Source:** Staff welfare schemes covered under “Staff Welfare Fund” (For Serving & Retired Employees). (ws-benifit-welfare-fund.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High

## 4. What financial help is given to the family of an employee who dies during service?
**Answer:** Financial assistance includes funeral expenses of ₹10,000, compassionate appointment for family members, and bravery awards of ₹20 lakhs for officers or ₹10 lakhs for clerical staff.

**Source:** Financial assistance to the family of deceased on account of death during the service period under bank’s different staff welfare schemes. (ws-emp-dec.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High

## 5. Which hospitals are empanelled for retirees' health check-ups?
**Answer:** Manipal Hospitals Dwarka and Ayushman Hospital, Super Speciality Hospital, are empanelled for retirees' health check-ups.

**Source:** EMPANELMENT WITH MANIPAL SUPER SPECIALITY HOSPITAL FOR HEALTH CHECK UP (EMAIL.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High

## 6. What does the IBA Group Medical Insurance Scheme for retired employees cover?
**Answer:** The IBA Group Medical Insurance Scheme for retired employees covers medical expenses for retired officers/employees and their spouses.

**Source:** IBA’S GROUP MEDICAL INSURANCE POLICY FOR RETIRED EMPLOYEES - EXTENSION OF DATE UPTO 20.11.2017 (HOSP_NOTICE 03 11 2017.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High

## 7. Is domiciliary treatment covered under the retiree medical insurance scheme?
**Answer:** Yes, domiciliary treatment is covered under the retiree medical insurance scheme as it is defined as medical treatment for an illness/disease/injury which would normally require hospital care but is taken at home.

**Source:** Medical Insurance Scheme as per 10th Bipartite Settlement/Joint Note Dated 25.05.2015 - Retired Officers/Workmen (Med_Ins_Sch.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High

## 8. Can a retiree take a loan to pay the medical insurance premium?
**Answer:** Yes, a retiree can take a demand loan to meet the insurance premium amount for the IBA’s Group Medical Insurance Scheme.

**Source:** HRMD CIRCULAR NO 335– Personal loan to retirees for payment of IBA’s Group Medical Insurance Premium for the policy period 01.11.2016 to 31.10.2017 (335.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High

## 10. How is a deceased account holder's claim settled?
**Answer:** The claim is settled based on nomination or survivorship clause, with payment to the nominee or survivor after establishing identity and death through documents, provided no court order restrains payment.

**Source:** Code of Bank's Commitment to Customers (PNB) (CODE_OF_BANKS_COMMITMENT_TO_CUSTOMERS_ADOPTED_BY_PNB.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High

## 11. How can a customer file a complaint or grievance with the bank?
**Answer:** A customer can file a complaint by following the bank's internal procedures, which include guidance on how to make a complaint, where to make it, and to whom it should be addressed. The bank will also inform the customer about the expected timeline for a reply and the options for escalation if the outcome is unsatisfactory.

**Source:** Code of Bank's Commitment to Customers (PNB) (CODE_OF_BANKS_COMMITMENT_TO_CUSTOMERS_ADOPTED_BY_PNB.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High

## 12. What are the customer's rights under PNB's Customer Rights Policy?
**Answer:** The customer's rights under PNB's Customer Rights Policy include the right to fair treatment without unfair discrimination, the right to clear and simple information about products and services, and the right to be informed about key risks and terms.

**Source:** Customer Rights Policy (ho_cust_care_customer_rights_policy.pdf)  ·  **Confidence:** High
