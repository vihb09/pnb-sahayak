# PNB Sahayak — Knowledge Base Guide (what's in the 56 documents)

All documents are **real, public PNB files** (HRMS staff portal + pnbindia.in).
They fall into 6 themes. The assistant answers only from these.

---

## A. Pension & Life Certificate (7 documents)
Rules for pensioners — chiefly the annual **Life Certificate** (proof the pensioner is
alive, needed to keep pension flowing), plus pension tax and a pension option.

| Document | What it says |
|----------|--------------|
| LC_10_2019_FINAL_CIRCULAR | Core rule: staff/family pensioners submit a Life Certificate **every November**. |
| CIRCULAR12_24102018 | Earlier circular on submitting the Life Certificate. |
| PFPENSION102020-merged | COVID extension — submission allowed Oct–Dec 2020. |
| PPF132020final | Further extension of Life Certificate submission until Feb 2021. |
| Lifecertificate | The Life Certificate form itself. |
| Investment | **TDS (tax) on pension income** for FY2019-20 and recording investment proofs in HRMS. |
| Cir 06-18-Pension option to CRS employees | 2nd option of pension for compulsorily-retired officers/employees. |

## D. IBA Group Medical Insurance Scheme for retirees (largest theme, ~26 documents)
The medical insurance scheme for retired officers/employees — yearly **options, renewals,
extensions, policy documents, operational guidelines, domiciliary coverage, claim forms**.
The many files are circulars across 2015–2020 for successive policy years.
Key ones:
| Document | What it says |
|----------|--------------|
| Med_Ins_Sch | The scheme per the 10th Bipartite Settlement (2015) — the founding document *(OCR'd)*. |
| RETIREE 17-18 WITH / WITHOUT DOMICILIARY | Full policy wording, with and without domiciliary cover. |
| REIREE POLICY FOR MEDICAL INSURANCEPNB | Tailormade Group Mediclaim policy (retirees). |
| 395 … OPERATIONAL GUIDELINES-RETIREES | How the scheme runs day-to-day. |
| OPERATIONAL GUIDELINES DOMICILIARY | Reimbursement of **domiciliary** (at-home) treatment claims. |
| DOMICILIARY DISEASES / 333 DATED 13.10.16 | List of diseases eligible for domiciliary cover. |
| PNB-RETIRE POLICY SUPER TOP UP | "Super Top-Up" extra cover policy. |
| 335 | **Personal loan to retirees** to pay the insurance premium. |
| NOTICE 09 11 2017 | TPA (claims administrator) changed to Health Insurance TPA of India. |
| CLAIM FORM | The insurance claim form. |
| 515/2020, 422/425 (2018-19), 378 (2017-18), DOMI/WITHOUT_DOMI (2016-17), 31.08.2016 (2015-16), hosp_30082017, 09.12.2016, NOTICE 05.12.16, NOTICE_29_11_2017, HOSP_NOTICE, hospitalisation_notice, 300 bc, OPERATIONAL GUIDELINES | Year-by-year **options / renewals / date-extensions / guidelines** for the scheme. |

---

**Coverage note:** the knowledge base is strongest on **retiree medical insurance, pension/life-
certificate, and staff welfare**, plus solid **customer policy** coverage (KYC, account opening,
customer rights, deceased claims, grievances, NRO). Questions outside these areas are honestly
declined and escalated.
